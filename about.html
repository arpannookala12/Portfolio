About Me

My Journey into IoT, Data Science, and Advanced AI

My Background & Education

I am currently pursuing an M.S. in Data Science at Rutgers University (expected graduation: May 2025). My entry point into the world of engineering began with a Bachelor's in Electronics Engineering, during which I discovered a love for sensor-based IoT projects and analytics. Over time, I pivoted toward more advanced Machine Learning, culminating in experiences with deep reinforcement learning and generative models.

  • Rutgers University: M.S. in Data Science (2023 – 2025)
  • Undergraduate: Electronics Engineering

My Philosophy

I believe the most impactful Data Scientists are those who can bridge complex theoretical knowledge and real-world practicality— whether that’s building a pot-in-pot refrigerator with IoT temperature logging or deploying a full-scale DRL algorithm in finance or traffic control. My goal is to design, implement, and deploy solutions that are both innovative and genuinely impactful.
