About Me

I’m a data scientist with a strong background in advanced data analysis and Machine Learning driven by a passion for bridging academic research and real-world solutions. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new data visualization tools, diving into NLP libraries, and staying updated on the latest AI breakthroughs. If you’re interested in collaborative research, innovative ML solutions, or just chatting about data, feel free to reach out!
